Our Story

Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre Students Organisation

EASTCSO (Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre Students Organisation) is the special organisation established in order to deal with students affairs directly at EASTC.

The organisation was established to bridge the gap between students and administration, ensuring that student voices are heard and student welfare is prioritised. EASTCSO coordinates academic activities, social events, sports, and welfare programs for all enrolled students at EASTC.

Under the motto "Serving Students Through Students," the organisation strives to create an environment where every student can thrive academically and socially. The 2025/2026 executive organ is led by President Mario Macha and a dedicated team of ministers and secretaries spanning all key areas of student life.

Our Mission

To coordinate and support student activities, events, announcements, and communication between students and EASTC administration for the benefit of all learners.

Our Vision

To be a leading student organisation in Eastern Africa that empowers students through excellent leadership, welfare services, and academic support programs.

Core Values

Integrity, excellence, inclusivity, transparency, and service — these are the values that guide every decision and activity of EASTCSO.

What We Offer

Student Services

EASTCSO provides a wide range of services to support student life, welfare, and academic success.

Our Services

How We Support Students

Academic Support

Tutoring sessions, study groups, academic resources, and guidance on course selection and career pathways in statistics and data science.

Health & Welfare

Student health awareness programs, welfare clinics, mental health support, and coordination with EASTC's health services for student wellbeing.

Sports & Recreation

Wasomi League football, sports tournaments, recreational activities, and entertainment events to keep students active and engaged.

Communication

Official announcements, notices, and information dissemination between EASTC administration and students through various channels.

Student Representation

Representing student interests to the administration, advocating for improved facilities, resources, and policy changes that benefit all learners.

Leadership Development

Leadership training programs, workshops, seminars, and opportunities for students to develop governance and management skills.

Financial Guidance

Assistance with scholarship information, loan guidance, HESLB matters, and financial planning support for students.

Transport & Logistics

Coordination of student transport needs, field trips, institutional visits, and logistics for student events and activities.

Social & Community

Community service initiatives, social events, cultural programs, and activities that foster unity and camaraderie among EASTC students.
