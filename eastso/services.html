What We Offer

Student Services

EASTCSO provides a wide range of services to support your academic journey and campus life.

Available Services

All Services

Academic Support

Resources and peer programs to help you excel in your studies at EASTC.

  • Peer tutoring sessions
  • Study group coordination
  • Past papers & resources
  • Academic counselling

Student Welfare

Supporting your health, wellbeing, and financial needs throughout your studies.

  • Health referral services
  • Mental health support
  • Bursary applications
  • Emergency assistance

Sports & Recreation

Stay active and compete with your fellow students across multiple sports.

  • Football & volleyball teams
  • Basketball & table tennis
  • Athletics programme
  • Inter-faculty tournaments

Cultural Activities

Celebrate East African heritage and express your creativity on campus.

  • Annual cultural week
  • Drama & theatre club
  • Music & dance groups
  • Art exhibitions

Career Development

Build the skills and connections you need for a successful career after EASTC.

  • CV writing workshops
  • Annual career fair
  • Alumni networking
  • Internship partnerships

Communications

Stay informed and connected with the EASTC student community at all times.

  • Student newsletter
  • WhatsApp broadcast updates
  • Notice board management
  • Digital announcements

Library & Resources

Access academic materials and study spaces to support your learning.

  • Library advocacy
  • Digital resource access
  • Study room booking
  • Textbook sharing scheme

Community Outreach

Give back to the community and develop your leadership through service.

  • Volunteer programmes
  • Community statistics workshops
  • Environmental initiatives
  • Charity drives

How to Access Services

All EASTCSO services are available to registered student members. Visit the EASTCSO office or contact the relevant committee representative.

Office hours: Mon–Fri, 9 AM – 4 PM

Become a Member

Service Updates

  • Welfare office: drop-in Tuesdays & Thursdays
  • Library open late during the exam period
  • Sports training: weekdays, 6–7:30 AM
